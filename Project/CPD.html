Continuing Professional Development (CPD) Provider

On July 12, 2017, The Department of Computer Engineering at De La Salle University received approval from the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) and was designated as a Local Continuing Professional Development Provider with Accreditation No. 2017-017.

In accordance with Resolution No. 2016-990, which mandates registered and licensed computer engineering professionals to accumulate educational credits to enhance their expertise, referred to as the Revised Guidelines on the Continuing Professional Development (CPD) program.
