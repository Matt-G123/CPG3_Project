ASSOCIATION OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING STUDENTS (ACCESS)

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACCESS) is a professional organization under De La Salle University – Council of Student Organizations (DLSU-CSO) that consists mainly of Computer Engineering students who aspire to be Lasallian achievers. In order to attain this, the organization offers professional and educational activities that guide each student’s academic and career-related concerns.

Overall, ACCESS perseveres to nurture and strengthen the bond of each student to promote unity and camaraderie throughout the organization as a stepping stone for their future success.

Vision

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACCESS) envisions it to be an organization that caters to the needs of its members. It aims to be a breeding ground for students who aim to become professionals and pioneers in the field of Computer Engineering, while upholding the Lasallian values in their daily actions.

Mission

The association strives to enhance technological knowledge and engineering skills in the domain of Computer Engineering to prepare the students for the competitive industry. The organization also intends to instill social awareness and understanding simultaneously promoting camaraderie of Computer Engineering to nurture true Lasallian achievers.

ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATIONS ENGINEERING SOCIETY (ECES)

The Electronics and Communications Engineering Society (ECES), an affiliate of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and the Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines – Manila Student Chapter (IECEP-MSC), is the premier academic organization that caters to the Electronics Engineering Student Body of De La Salle University. In its 43 years of operation, and the recognition of being a three-time best professional organization in De La Salle University, ECES is able to provide its members with both academic and industrial support – with services ranging from the provision and conduct of tutorials to the hosting of industrial immersion seminars to provide the ECE aspirants of DLSU with an edge once they enter the industry as professionals.

Vision

The Electronics and Communications Engineering Society (ECES) is a premier organization, unified by proactive Christian Achievers, and committed to forging future leaders in technology and innovation for God and country.

Mission

The Electronics and Communications Engineering Society is the leading professional organization for aspiring ECE majors of De La Salle University, Manila. We strive to ensure the holistic development of our members by offering exceptional scholastic programs and services accompanied by outstanding and innovative collaborations with external organizations, honing academically excellent and professionally competent Electronics and Communication Engineers pioneering social transformation.
